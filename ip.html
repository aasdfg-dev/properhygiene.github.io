Healthy Respiratory Hygiene


Today’s generation have been facing several difficulties, it is not deprived from everyone’s prior knowledge that there’s current existence of a respiratory virus called Novel Coronavirus (nCov) which was originated from Wuhan, China.
The said virus is transmitted through physical contact and thus, is not by airborne. This, and other various diseases were easy to prevent; by acquiring a healthy respiratory hygiene.




Let us first define Hygiene.

Hygiene

-is the science concerned with the prevention of illness and maintenance of health.

What is Respiratory Hygiene?

Respiratory hygiene

-is intended to build awareness of signs of illness that could indicate someone,
either a patient or a member of the dental team, has a respiratory infection.

What is Heathy Respiratory Hygiene?

-A Heathy Respiratory Hygiene is the health maintenance of your respiratory system,
it is the etiquette that most people do to prevent themselves from acquiring various diseases
and viruses from the environment that they live in.


You are now aware on what is hygiene, respiratory hygiene and healthy respiratory hygiene,
now let us add some additional knowledge, these are some respiratory diseases/viruses:


*Asthma
*Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
*Chronic Bronchitis
*Emphysema
*Lung Cancer
*Cystic Fibrosis/Bronchiectasis
*Pneumonia
*Pleural Effusion
*croup
*sinusitis
*meningitis (inflammation of the lining of the brain and spinal cord)
*encephalitis (inflammation of the brain)
*Novel Coronavirus (nCov)



These are the most commonly observed symptoms of respiratory infection:

*Coughing
*Congestion
*Runny nose
*Sneezing
*Increased production of respiratory secretions
*fever
*sore throat
*headache
*muscle aches
*fatigue
*malaise (feeling unwell).




Common respiratory hygiene wherever you might be:


1.When you sneeze or cough cover your mouth with tissue.

2.While coughing or sneezing avoid covering it with your hands; use your elbow rather.

3.Use a face/surgical mask to prevent other people from acquiring respiratory infection.

4.Never forget to wash your hands properly.







This is how you do a proper handwashing.
